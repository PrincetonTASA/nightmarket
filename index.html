FAQ

what, really, is a night market?
Common throughout all of Taiwan, they attract crowds of people to their intricate alleys full of cheap and delicious food stands and shops. You can find the latest styles of clothing, cute accessories, and even glasses and phone cases for the lowest prices (if you know how to haggle.)

that sounds awesome! do I need to buy tickets?
Night Market tickets (to exchange for food - entry is free!) will be sold at table 1 in Frist, 4/7 - 4/10 from 8 to 10 pm. You get 2 free tickets with your PUID!

do you have anything else planned for tickets?
Good thing you asked! We will be holding a RAFFLE for a chance to win extra tickets! (And the free food that comes with it!!!) To enter, all you have to do is take a picture of yourself with your Night Market ticket(s) at the TASA table in Frist, and then upload the picture on Facebook (by posting on this page and/or tagging Princeton TASA).
