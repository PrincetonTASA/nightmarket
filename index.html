Have you heard of Taiwan's famous night markets? Common throughout all of Taiwan, they attract crowds of people to their intricate alleys full of cheap and delicious food stands and shops. You can find the latest styles of clothing, cute accessories, and even glasses and phone cases for the lowest prices (if you know how to haggle.)

How do I get in?
 Entry is free! Come visit for the performances or for the food.

So how do I get food?
 Glad you asked! You can get food from any booth in exchange for tickets.

OK, sounds cool—so what's a ticket?
 Nothing too special, it's just a slip of paper! Maybe you ought to ask how you get tickets!

Fair enough. How do I get tickets?
 Tickets will be sold at table 1 in Frist, 4/7 - 4/10 from 8 to 10 pm. You get 2 free tickets with your PUID!
Also, we will be holding a RAFFLE for a chance to win extra tickets! (And the free food that comes with it!!!) To enter, all you have to do is take a picture of yourself with your Night Market ticket(s) at the TASA table in Frist, and then upload the picture on Facebook (by posting on the event page and/or tagging Princeton TASA).
